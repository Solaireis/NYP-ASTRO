Just as colours are made more dramatic in sunsets on Earth. Sunsets on Mars, according to NASA, will appear bluish to our naked eye watching on Mars. The reason for this is due to fine dust particles which makes the blue near the Sun's part of the sky much more visible. However, in normal daylight, Mars Daylight is the familiar rusty dust color when viewed from the human eye.
