What do you do during your sessions?


We host space themed sharings weekly. Examples includes Evolution of Earth, Life of a Star, Black Holes etc. After the sharing members will test their learning through a competitive kahoot game or a break out room discussions to work in a team to share their ideas.

The theme of the event is chosen based on the space map where we will talk about space topics as we travel further away from the solar system.



If session is conducted physically, we do have Star gazing sessions with our members where we will see the beautiful night sky. Examples of photos taken from NYP is shown below.
